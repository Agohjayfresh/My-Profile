👉 WHAT BENUE TECH SKILLS IS ALL ABOUT 👈

Benue Tech Skills is a platform that is created by the Benue State Government in collaboration with other National and international bodies in the ICT firm to train and empower the youths of Benue State in the different areas of ICT to bridge the gap between lack of skill force and man power in our innovative world of today.
